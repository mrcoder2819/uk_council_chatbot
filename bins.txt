BIN COLLECTION AND MISSED BIN COLLECTION - UK

Local councils are responsible for waste collection.

MISSED BIN COLLECTION

If your bin was missed:

Step 1:
Go to:
https://www.gov.uk/missed-bin-collection

Step 2:
Enter your postcode.

Step 3:
GOV.UK will send you to your local council website.

Step 4:
Report the missed bin collection on your council website.

Before reporting, check:
- the bin was put out on the correct collection day
- the bin was outside before the required time
- the correct bin was used
- the bin was not contaminated
- the bin was not too heavy
- there were no council service delays

ORDER NEW BIN

Step 1:
Go to your local council website.

Step 2:
Search "order new bin" or "request replacement bin".

Step 3:
Fill in the council request form.
