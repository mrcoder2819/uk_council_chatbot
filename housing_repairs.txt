HOUSING REPAIRS AND MOULD - COUNCIL PROPERTY

If you live in a council house and have mould, damp, leaks, heating problems, or structural repair issues:

Step 1:
Report the problem directly to your local council housing repairs team.

Step 2:
Use GOV.UK to find your local council:
https://www.gov.uk/find-local-council

Step 3:
On your council website, search:
- report housing repair
- council house mould
- damp and mould
- emergency repair

Step 4:
If the council does not fix the issue, make a formal complaint:
https://www.gov.uk/council-housing/complaints

Important:
There is no single GOV.UK form for reporting repairs in all council houses.
Each council has its own housing repair page.

Emergency repairs:
Call your council directly if there is:
- no heating
- major water leak
- electrical danger
- unsafe structure
- serious damp or mould affecting health
